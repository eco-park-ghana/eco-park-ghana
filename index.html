Project overview

A family-focused eco-park built for growth.

The project is designed as an affordable, high-volume visitor destination in a tourism region of Ghana. It combines eco-tourism, recreation, and light adventure into a practical launch model that can expand into a larger destination over time.

Phase 1 focuses on the core revenue drivers: electric boat rides, quad biking, seating and picnic areas, basic visitor facilities, and a clean brand presence that appeals to families, schools, and weekend tourists from Accra.
